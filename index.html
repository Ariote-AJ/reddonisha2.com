Tax Planning & Consulting

Strategic tax advisory to help minimize risks, maximize efficiency, and ensure full compliance for your business. Expert representation in cases of audits, disputes, or regulatory queries.

  • Tax diagnostics & health reviews
  • Dispute resolution & mediation
  • Custom tax strategies
  • Cross-border and expatriate tax advice

VAT Remittance

Hassle-free calculation, documentation, and submission of Monthly VAT returns—keeping your business in good standing with authorities.

  • Accurate VAT computations and filing
  • Compliance checks
  • Timely submission reminders

PAYE Processing

Seamless payroll tax processing and representation—ensuring your staff payroll complies with all statutory obligations without error.

  • Pension & NSITF integration
  • Employee records management

Annual Returns & Filing

Complete preparation and submission of annual returns (including zero returns for exempt companies) quickly and accurately.

  • FIRS and state returns included
  • Document management & archiving

Tax Clearance Processing

Fast, reliable advice and documentation for obtaining business & personal tax clearance certificates—essential for contracts and compliance.

  • Requirements checklist
  • Follow-up with authorities
  • Renewal support

Auditing & Assurance

Rigorous reviews of accounting systems and controls to ensure reliability, security, and transparency. Improve your reputation and stakeholder confidence.

  • Internal & external audit
  • Risk assessment
  • Control recommendations

Financial Accounting

Preparation of end-of-year accounts for limited, public & enterprise businesses, and financial statement advisory.

  • IFRS compliance
  • Management accounts for business decision-making

Company Registration (CAC)

End-to-end facilitation for business incorporation and statutory registrations at the Corporate Affairs Commission.

  • Name reservation & filing
  • Advice on appropriate business type (Ltd, NGO, etc.)
  • Post-incorporation support
